Full bio ~130 words

Mirza Beširović runs product for Zendesk AI Agents, the company's flagship AI automation platform. With his team he has scaled it to $100M+ ARR and 4× the customer base: autonomous customer service for Netflix, Vimeo, Levi's, Reddit, and thousands of others, automating 80%+ of interactions across messaging, email, and voice. He leads the global product organization behind the platform and helped shape its commercial model: Zendesk was the first CX vendor to price AI per automated resolution. Before Zendesk he built AI and automation platforms at Ultimate.ai and Dixa, through hypergrowth and two M&A integrations. He started his career as a translator and interpreter, and writes the newsletter You Are the Product. He lives in Berlin.
